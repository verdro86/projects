Drawing:

  • Click and drag inside the canvas to draw.
  • Click "Clear Canvas" to fully erase the canvas.
  • "Pen Color" changes the color of the pen.
  • Click "Eraser" to erase parts of the current drawing.
    • To switch out of the eraser, select a pen color.
  • "Pen Width" changes the thickness of the pen.
  • Adjust the size of the canvas by using the "small","medium", and "large" buttons.

Recording and Replay:

  • "Start Recording" begins the recording.
  • "Stop Recording" ends the recording.
  • To play the recent recording, click "Play" after stoping a recording.
  • To save a recording, click "Save Recording" then the recording will be added into the "Saved Recordings" dropdown.
  • To replay a saved recording, choose a recording on the "Saved Recordings" dropdown then click play.
  • Adjust the playback speed using the slider.
    • Either adjust the playback speed prior to playing the recording, or while the recording is playing.
  • To delete a recording, select the recording on the "Saved Recordings" dropdown then clic "Delete".
